When Words Fail, Music Speaks

Music has been, and always will be, a very important part of my life. It gets me through the best of times and the worst of times. But of course, when I say 'Music', I don't mean the pop and electronic computerized stuff that you hear on the radio today. What I mean is pure music, you know, with genuine instruments. Some of my favourite artists include Bon Jovi, AC/DC, The Beatles, Shania Twain, N*SYNc, Led Zeppelin, Aerosmith an Linkin Park.

Rock N' Roll

I was born in the late 90's, but my heart has always belonged to the Rock n Roll music culture of the 70's and 80's. The sound of the drums and the guitar are the elixir of my life.

Rock Music is important to me because it allows me to escape this crazy world. It helps me to face up to the world.
